Pesto Pasta

Description

This is a Pasta Pesto recipe. It is a very easy dish to make with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients :

  • Whatever type of pasta you like
  • Pine nuts
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps :

  1. Boil the water with the pasta
  2. Blend the pine nuts, basil, olive oil, parmesan, salt and pepper
  3. Serve the pasta, and smash that sauce in.
